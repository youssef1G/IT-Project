Mazda RX-Vision

The Mazda RX-Vision is a concept car unveiled by Mazda at the 2015 Tokyo Motor Show. This sleek, two-door coupe showcases Mazda's vision for a future sports car powered by a next-generation rotary engine, the SKYACTIV-R. The RX-Vision embodies Mazda's commitment to innovation and its "never-stop-challenging" spirit. Design-wise, the RX-Vision features a long hood, low stance, and rear-wheel-drive layout, reflecting Mazda's KODO design philosophy. The compact rotary engine allows for a lower hood line and a more aggressive profile.
