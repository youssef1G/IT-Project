Porsche is a German company specializing in the production of luxury sports cars, and it is one of the most renowned brands in the world. Porsche was founded in 1931 by "Ferdinand Porsche," and its first cars appeared in 1948. The company produces luxury sports cars such as the "Porsche 911," "Porsche Cayenne," and "Porsche Panamera." Porsche is known for combining high performance with luxury, and it is famous for advanced engine technology and sophisticated all-wheel-drive systems. The company is also well-known for its participation in motorsports, including the 24 Hours of Le Mans races.
